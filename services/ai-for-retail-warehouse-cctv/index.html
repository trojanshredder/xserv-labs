What we build

Computer vision use cases that actually pay back.

We build computer vision deployments through both custom services and Praxate (our own AI-on-CCTV product). The capabilities below are what we ship in production — not lab demos.

Shelf monitoring & stockout alerts

Cameras pointed at shelves continuously assess empty or low slots. Real-time alerts to floor staff. Aggregated trend dashboards by SKU, store, time-of-day.

Footfall counting & demographics

Entry-camera counts people in / out, with rough demographic estimation. Conversion rate = transactions / footfall. Campaign measurement.

Queue management at checkout

Real-time queue length detection. Alerts when queue exceeds threshold. Aggregated data for staff scheduling.

PPE compliance for warehouses

Helmet, vest, mask, glove detection in PPE-required zones. Non-compliance events logged with timestamp and clip. Audit trail for safety regulators and insurers.

Headcount & zone occupancy

Real-time headcount by zone. Heatmaps of foot traffic. Safety-capacity compliance. Productivity-zone occupancy.

After-hours intrusion alerts

Zones + time windows defined. Person detected outside permitted hours? WhatsApp alert in seconds with video clip attached.

Common questions

Direct answers.

Will the AI run on our existing CCTV cameras?

In 95% of cases yes. Standard IP cameras supporting RTSP — the default for any modern Indian CCTV install — work for most use cases. We do a free site survey to confirm which feeds will work and which need repositioning.

Does video go to the cloud?

No, by default. We process video on-prem on a small appliance at your site. Only structured events (alerts, attendance, headcount) sync to dashboards. This addresses both bandwidth costs and privacy concerns common in Indian retail / warehouse deployments.

How much does deployment cost?

Per-site: ₹1.5L–₹4L for the on-prem appliance, plus per-camera-per-month software typically ₹500–₹2,500. ROI for shelf monitoring and queue management is usually under 6 months for mid-format retail.

Can you also do AI-based theft detection?

Yes — behaviour analytics for suspicious patterns + self-checkout monitoring for scan-mismatch detection. Important caveat: system flags should triage to trained loss-prevention staff, not act as automatic accusations. Privacy by design matters here.
