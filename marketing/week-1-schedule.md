## Pre-flight checklist for every post

Before scheduling, confirm:

- [ ] First three lines hook (would I click "see more" if I saw this in my feed?)
- [ ] One specific opinion or observation, not a generalisation
- [ ] No banned phrases (see strategy doc ground rules)
- [ ] Length appropriate (LinkedIn ~3000 char hard limit, ~1500 char for full visibility without "see more")
- [ ] Image or carousel attached if applicable
- [ ] External links moved to first comment, not in post body
- [ ] Tagged any specific accounts that should see it
- [ ] Hashtags lowercase, no more than 3-5

---

## Engagement plan for each post

Within first 30 minutes of post going live:

- [ ] Distribution Lead pings team channel
- [ ] 3-5 team members like and comment
- [ ] Founder DMs to 2-3 strategic contacts
- [ ] First-comment link added if applicable

Within first hour:

- [ ] Reply to every comment that arrives
- [ ] If 5+ comments by 30-min mark, lead engagement push (more DMs, more reposts)
