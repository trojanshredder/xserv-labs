# Week 2 video script — "The single best engineering interview question"

**Length target:** 45 seconds
**Format:** Selfie video
**Account:** Founder personal

---

## Script

```
If you only have time for one question in an engineering interview, here is the one I use now.

I open one of our actual codebases, find a bug we hit recently, and ask the candidate to walk me through how they would investigate it.

That is the whole question. Not "what is the answer." How they investigate.

What I am watching for: do they read the code carefully, or do they jump to conclusions? Do they ask clarifying questions, or do they pretend they understand? When they hit something they do not know, do they say so, or do they keep going and hope?

You learn more about an engineer's actual work style in 20 minutes of this than in two hours of leetcode puzzles.

We hired three people last year using mostly this question. All three are still here. All three are good. The signal is real.

That's it.
```

Same as week 1. Natural light, phone at eye level, look at the lens. Read through once, then say it.
