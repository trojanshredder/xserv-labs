## Total deliverable

- 20 text posts
- 4 carousels (8-12 slides each)
- 4 video scripts (45-60 seconds each)

Total: 28 pieces of content for 30 days of execution.

---

## Variations and reuse

If a slot's content doesn't fit when the day arrives (e.g., something topical happens, a real client moment to write about), substitute. The schedule is a backbone, not a contract.

Every text post should be repurposed as a carousel 6-8 weeks later. Every carousel can be reposted as individual slide images. Every video can be transcribed and become a text post.
