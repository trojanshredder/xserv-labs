What it does

Everything a fleet manager does — just faster, cheaper, and never tired.

Most fleet software is either a glorified GPS tracker or an enterprise behemoth. Traxium sits exactly where Indian transporters actually live — between the spreadsheet and SAP.

Live GPS tracking

Every truck, every 10 seconds, on one map. Geofences, halt alerts, ETA recalculation as routes change. Works with most India-market GPS devices.

AI delay prediction

Traxium watches weather, traffic patterns, and the truck's own history to predict delays before they happen — so customers get told first, not last.

Fuel audits

Mileage tracking by vehicle and driver. Anomaly detection for theft, pilferage, and false bills. Save 5–12% on fuel in the first 90 days for most fleets.

Compliance alerts

RC, insurance, fitness, PUC, permits — all expiry-tracked. Alerts before fines, not after. One missed permit pays for the whole subscription.

WhatsApp alerts

Real-time updates straight to drivers, customers, and managers — on the app they already use. No new apps to install for anyone in the field.

Auto-invoicing, GST-ready

Trip-to-invoice in seconds. GSTIN-aware, place-of-supply correct, IRN-ready, e-way bill linked. Books-friendly exports to Tally and Zoho.

Common questions

The ones we get on every demo call.

What is Traxium?

Traxium is an AI-powered fleet management platform built for Indian trucking and logistics businesses. It combines live GPS tracking, AI-driven delay prediction, fuel audits, compliance alerts, and GST-ready invoicing in one dashboard — so transporters don't need five different tools to run their fleet.

Who is Traxium built for?

Trucking SMEs, transporters, logistics service providers, and any business that runs 5 to 500+ trucks in India. It's especially built for fleet owners who've outgrown spreadsheets and basic GPS-only solutions but don't want enterprise-pricing complexity.

Does Traxium work with my existing GPS devices?

In most cases yes. Traxium supports the major GPS device protocols used in India (Teltonika, Concox, Meitrack, Aquila, and others). If you already have devices fitted, we can usually onboard them without replacing hardware. If you need devices, we help you source them at fair India-market rates.

How is Traxium different from other fleet management software?

Three things. It's built India-first (GST invoicing, e-way bill, WhatsApp alerts, multi-language). It's priced for SMEs, not just enterprises. And the AI actually does work — delay prediction, fuel audits, anomaly detection — instead of just being a marketing line.

How much does Traxium cost?

Plans start at low monthly per-vehicle pricing with volume discounts. There's a free trial and no setup fee. Pricing is on the Traxium website — or talk to us for a custom enterprise quote.

Is Traxium GST compliant?

Yes. Invoices are GST-compliant out of the box, with auto-numbering, place-of-supply logic, IRN generation for e-invoicing, and e-way bill integration where applicable.

Can we integrate Traxium with our existing accounting software?

Yes. Native exports to Tally and Zoho Books are built in. APIs are available for custom integrations with SAP, NetSuite, custom ERPs, and warehouse systems.

Who owns my data?

You do. Your data is yours, exportable any time, and we'll never sell or share it. Servers in India, GDPR-aware practices, and DPA available on request.
