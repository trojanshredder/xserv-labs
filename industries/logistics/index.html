What we build for logistics

The platforms that run a logistics business — not the ones that demo well.

Logistics tech in India fails for the same reason every quarter: vendors build for what the buyer says they want, not for what the dispatcher, the driver, and the accounts team actually do. We start from the warehouse floor and the truck cabin and work back.

Fleet management platforms

Live GPS, route optimization, delay prediction, fuel audits, driver behaviour, compliance. From single-yard fleets to 500+ truck operations.

Transport management (TMS)

Order to delivery to invoice. Dispatch planning, freight rate engines, multi-modal coordination, customer portals, automated billing.

Warehouse management (WMS)

Pick-pack-dispatch, putaway, cycle counts, multi-location inventory, barcode/RFID, returns. For cold chain, food, pharma, e-commerce, industrial.

Last-mile & delivery apps

Rider apps, dispatch consoles, customer tracking pages, proof of delivery, COD reconciliation. Offline-first, India-tested in poor-signal areas.

E-way bill & GST automation

Auto-generate e-way bills from dispatch, IRN-ready invoicing, place-of-supply logic, RCM. Integrates into your books or stands alone.

GPS & IoT integrations

Teltonika, Concox, Meitrack, Aquila and others — supported on day one. Custom integration for hardware we haven't seen, included in scope.

Common questions

What logistics buyers ask us first.

What kind of logistics software do you build?

Fleet management, transport management systems (TMS), warehouse management (WMS), last-mile delivery, dock management, dispatch planners, GPS integrations, e-way bill automation, freight billing and reconciliation. If logistics teams use it, we've built one.

Do you have an off-the-shelf product?

Yes — Traxium, our AI-powered fleet management platform for Indian trucking SMEs. Live GPS, delay prediction, fuel audits, GST invoicing, WhatsApp alerts. Free trial available. For everything Traxium doesn't cover, we build custom.

Can you integrate with our existing GPS, ERP and TMS?

Yes. We've integrated with most major Indian GPS device protocols (Teltonika, Concox, Meitrack), with Tally, SAP, Zoho, Oracle, the e-way bill API, GST APIs, Razorpay, and bank statement APIs. Custom integrations are a normal part of every project.

How long does a logistics platform take to build?

An MVP (one core flow, e.g. dispatch + tracking) is typically 8–12 weeks. A full multi-module platform (fleet + TMS + billing + integrations) runs 16–28 weeks. Working software every two weeks throughout, not at the end.

What's a realistic budget?

Custom logistics platforms typically range ₹10L–₹60L for v1, depending on modules, integrations, and mobile apps required. We always scope before we quote. Or skip the build and start on Traxium for a fraction of that.

Can you work for international logistics customers?

Yes. We work with logistics companies across the US, UK, UAE, and Singapore. For US/UK we hold morning-overlap hours so meetings aren't at midnight.
