What we build for healthcare

The systems that keep clinical and operational teams focused on patients.

Healthcare software has a higher bar than most. It has to be available 24/7, never lose a record, work in clinical workflows that vary by department, and stay on the right side of compliance. We've built for hospitals, diagnostic chains, telehealth startups, and pharma companies — and we know each one's specific constraints.

HMS & clinic management

OPD, IPD, billing, pharmacy, stores, lab, radiology, blood bank, OT, doctor workflows. Built around real Indian hospital operations.

EMR / EHR systems

Patient records, vitals, prescriptions, treatment plans, e-Rx, lab results, imaging. Specialty templates for cardiology, ortho, gynae, paeds, ICU.

Telehealth platforms

Video consults, e-prescriptions, patient onboarding, scheduling, payments, follow-ups. WebRTC, Zoom, Daily.co — picked by use case.

Lab & diagnostic management

Common questions

What healthcare buyers ask first.

What healthcare software do you build?

HMS (hospital management), EMR/EHR (electronic medical/health records), telehealth platforms, lab/diagnostic management, pharmacy management, patient portals, doctor apps, claims and TPA workflows, and pharma supply chain tools.

Are you ABDM compliant?

We build ABDM-ready integrations — ABHA ID linkage, HIE-CM consent flows, health record interoperability via FHIR. We've shipped systems that pass NDHM milestone certifications.

Can the system run on-premise for hospitals that need it?

Yes. We deploy on-prem, hybrid, or cloud-hosted in Indian data centres. Many of our hospital clients require on-prem deployment for compliance and continuity reasons.

Do you integrate with existing HMS like Insta, Suvarna, eHospital?

Yes — we've integrated with most major Indian HMS systems via HL7, FHIR, and custom APIs. We can extend, integrate, or replace, depending on what your team actually needs.

What about patient data privacy?

DPDP Act compliant, HIPAA-aware, audit trails, role-based access, encryption at rest and in transit. We sign DPAs and custom security agreements as standard for healthcare clients.

How long does an HMS rollout take?

Single-clinic / small hospital MVP: 8–14 weeks. Multi-department hospital deployment: 14–28 weeks. Multi-site chain rollouts run in phases — first site live in 12 weeks, scale-out over 6–12 months.
