What we build for retail

The platforms that make a store, chain or brand actually run.

Most retail tech is one of two things: a beautiful storefront that doesn't know what's in stock, or a back-office system that the floor staff hates. We build the layer that ties everything together — one source of truth from POS to warehouse to D2C site.

When custom wins

Shopify is good. Until it isn't.

Stay on Shopify when

  • You sell standard SKUs to consumers in one country
  • Your custom logic fits inside Shopify Functions / extensions
  • Your team can manage the platform without engineering
  • App ecosystem covers your needs

Build custom when

  • Complex variants, configurators, bundles, subscriptions
  • B2B + D2C in one platform with different pricing tiers
  • India-specific tax, shipping, COD logic that apps half-cover
  • You want a single platform for POS + e-com + warehouse
  • International expansion with country-specific tax and payment logic
  • You're done paying per-transaction fees as you scale

Common questions

What retail buyers ask first.

Do you build custom POS systems?

Yes. Cloud-first POS with offline mode, multi-store, GST invoicing, payment gateway integration (Razorpay, PayU, PhonePe), loyalty, returns, and central reporting. Works on Android tablets, Windows POS, or any browser.

Can you integrate with Shopify, WooCommerce or Magento?

Yes. We're often hired to build the layer on top — custom POS, OMS, warehouse, shipping, returns, B2B portal — that the off-the-shelf storefront doesn't do well. Bi-directional sync with stock, orders, customers.

Do you build full D2C platforms from scratch?

Yes, when off-the-shelf doesn't fit — typically for brands with complex SKU/variant logic, B2B + D2C hybrid, subscription, or international expansion needs that Shopify Plus can't handle without painful workarounds.

Can you handle quick commerce / dark store operations?

Yes. Dark store ops, rider apps, dispatch routing, real-time stock, 10-minute promise SLAs. We've built and integrated with the systems quick commerce operators run.

What about Indian-specific logistics integrations?

Shiprocket, Delhivery, Blue Dart, Ekart, Ecom Express — integrated. NDR workflows, RTO, COD reconciliation, weight-disputes — handled in software.

How long does a retail platform build take?

Custom POS: 8–14 weeks. Full D2C platform: 14–24 weeks. Omnichannel OMS layer on top of existing storefronts: 10–16 weeks. We always scope before quoting.
